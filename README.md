# 🐉 A Song of Ice and Fire Archive 🐺

---

## 📖 Introduction
Welcome to the **A Song of Ice and Fire Archive**! This repository serves as a **fan archive of visual assets** related to the rich and expansive fantasy universe created by George R. R. Martin. Whether you're looking for reference material or just browsing, you'll find a highly structured repository dedicated to the lore of Westeros and Essos.

---

## 🗃️ What This Repository Contains
We aim to catalog everything from the known world. Inside, you'll find:

* 👤 **Character images**: From legendary heroes to cunning villains.
* 🗺️ **Maps of Westeros and Essos**: Exploring the vast geography of the known world.
* 🛡️ **House sigils and banners**: Representing the great and minor houses.
* 📚 **Book covers**: Various editions and international releases.
* 🐉 **Creatures**: Dragons, direwolves, krakens, and more.
* 🏰 **Locations**: Winterfell, King's Landing, The Wall, and beyond.
* 🗡️ **Weapons**: Valyrian steel swords and iconic armaments.
* 🎨 **Artwork and scenes**: Breathtaking depictions of iconic moments.

---

## 📁 Repository Structure
To keep things organized, especially given the immense number of characters, places, and items, we use an **A–Z folder system**. This helps categorize assets alphabetically, ensuring the repository remains clean and easily navigable.

Inside each letter folder (e.g., `A/`, `B/`, `C/`), you'll find the following subdirectories:

| Category | Description | Example Content |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| `characters/` | Portraits and character art | Jon Snow, Daenerys Targaryen |
| `locations/` | Castles, cities, and landmarks | Winterfell, Casterly Rock |
| `maps/` | Cartography of regions | Map of the North, Essos |
| `house-sigils/` | Coats of arms and banners | House Stark Direwolf |
| `book-covers/` | Novel covers and art | A Game of Thrones |
| `creatures/` | Beasts and monsters | Drogon, Ghost |
| `weapons/` | Swords, shields, and items | Ice, Longclaw |
| `artwork/` | Fan art and illustrations | The Red Wedding |
| `scenes/` | Important moments | Battle of the Bastards |

### Example
```text
A/
├── artwork/
├── book-covers/
├── characters/
├── creatures/
├── house-sigils/
├── locations/
├── maps/
├── scenes/
└── weapons/
```
